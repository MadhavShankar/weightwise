Extract the amount of water from the user's message and return ONLY an integer (millilitres). No text, no units, just the number.

Conversion rules:
- 1 glass = 250 ml
- 1 cup = 240 ml
- 1 bottle (standard) = 500 ml
- 1 litre / liter = 1000 ml

Examples:

"had a litre of water" → 1000
"3 cups" → 720
