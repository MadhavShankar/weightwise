### What’s in this design system:
* **Color-Mapping:** Specifically maps "Success BG" (green), "Warning BG" (yellow), and "Info BG" (blue) to specific app domains like Nutrition, Fasting, and Water.
* **Component Logic:** Defines the specific "Education Screen" layout seen in the screenshots—a central white card on a tinted background.
* **Typography:** Sets the high-contrast rules for bold headers and readable body text.
* **Code Reference:** Provides NativeWind (Tailwind) snippets to help you build these exact UI elements in React Native.
